About Me
After personal decision led me to uproot my life in Texas and move to Wisconsin in 2020. I decided that I was going to finally persue my interests in web developement. My relationship with coding began at the middle school age of 13 when my parents allowed me to have a MySpace page. I would spend hours on end refactoring existing layouts for fun. Without anyway to further develop those skills at the time that flame slowly dwindled. I went through school and went into the work force. Now back in Texas, I currently work in the health insurance industry doing customer service and while there is plenty of room for career growth I am ready to move on to explore career opportunities in a field that I have always loved. I am still learning but I am committed to this challenge and what it has to offer.
